# Incident Response Plan

We maintain a documented incident response plan that is tested via tabletop
exercise at least annually. The plan defines severity levels (SEV1-SEV4) and
target response times: SEV1 incidents require an initial response within 15
minutes and executive notification within 1 hour.

Customers are notified of any confirmed data breach affecting their data within
72 hours of confirmation, in line with applicable regulatory requirements.

A third party performs an annual penetration test against production systems,
and findings are remediated according to severity-based SLAs (critical: 7 days,
high: 30 days, medium: 90 days).
